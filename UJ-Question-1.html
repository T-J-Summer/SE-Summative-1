Europeans on UK Rails!

Unlike any country in the world, the UK has chosen to combine both the Imperial & Metric systems to have our own measurement system.

Throughout this journey, if you want to go back a step please use the back arrow on the top left of the page

With 98% or the world using the imperial system it would make sense for the UK to follow suit, unfortunately this isn't the case. This web app will help you to understand some of the strange quirks of our measurement system.
