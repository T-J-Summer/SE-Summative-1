Europeans on UK Rails!


The UK uses Litres or Millilitres to measure the volume of coffee consumed on trains
Fun Fact: over 432,000 Litres of coffee where sold on UK trains in January 2025!

The drivers have even more complication, as they buy fuel in Litres but then measure the economy in Miles Per Gallon (MPG)!!!
